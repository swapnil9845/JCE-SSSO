RESEARCH SCHOLAR

The department has been imparting quality education at undergraduate and post graduate level.The faculty members have been active in teaching and research activities.Add on courses are being conducted in addition to the regular curriculan,so as to keep abreast with the latest developments and emerging technologies.Various departmental laboratories have been provided with state of art equipment.
